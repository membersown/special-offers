  1. Offer commences 12.00am, Tuesday 1 November 2016 and closes 11.59pm (AEDT), Friday 23 December 2016 and may be extended.
  2. This offer is only available to new members who satisfy the eligibility criteria of Teachers Health Fund, whose membership application is received by Teachers Health Fund from 1 November 2016 and closes 11.59pm (AEDT), 23 December 2016.
  3. The new member must select Hospital and Extras cover, or a combined cover, in order to be eligible to receive this offer.
  4. The new member must provide the promotion code “SIX” when joining online, over the phone or with a Business Development officer.
  5. The new member must select a Direct Debit payment plan.
  6. Six (6) weeks free cover is applied to the second month of the new membership, upon successful receipt of the new member’s first contribution payment.
  7. The offer does not apply to stand-alone Hospital, Extras or Ambulance only policies.
  8. The offer does not apply to policy upgrades, or when a spouse or dependant is added to an existing Teachers Health Fund policy.
  9. This offer is only available to a new Teachers Health Fund policyholder who has not been a paying/financial member of Teachers Health Fund or UniHealth Insurance in the past four (4) months.
  10. The offer is not available to new members who have received an incentive offer from Teachers Health Fund or UniHealth Insurance within the last 12 months.
  11. Terms and conditions are subject to change without notice.
